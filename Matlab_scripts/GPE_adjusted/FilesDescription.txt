'GPE_adjusted_HorFloVel.m':
Matlab script used to plot the surrogate adjusted and full-complexity model outcomes.

'SR_bathymetry.mat':
Bathymetry of the Schwarzenbach reservoir.

'SurrogateMatrix_2.mat'
Outcomes of the surrogate model adjusted using the maximum likelihoods of calibration parameters according Scenario 0.

'HorizontalVelocityDomainDELFT3D.mat.mat'
Outcomes of the full-complexity model using the maximum likelihoods of calibration parameters according Scenario 0.
